Za'atar spiced chicken

Description

This middle eastern consists of za'atar spiced chicken breast with bulgar
stuffed tomatoes and a zingy lemon garlic sauce.

    Ingredients
  • 10 ounce chicken breast
  • 1 red onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 lemon
  • 3 roma tomato
  • 1/4 ounce parsley
  • 4 tblsp low fat plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp sour cream
  • chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup bulgar wheat
  • 1 tbsp za'atar
  • butter
  • salt and pepper
  • 1tbsp olive oil
      • Instructions
    1. Adjust rack to top position and preheat oven to 450 degrees. Wash and dry produce.
      • Halve, peel, and dice onion into ¾-inch-thick pieces; mince a few pieces until you have 2 TBSP
      . Peel and mince or grate garlic (reserve a pinch for step 3). Zest and quarter lemon. Halve tomatoes lengthwise; scoop out and discard
      seeds with a spoon. Pat insides dry with paper towels. Finely chop parsley.
    2. Heat a drizzle of oil in a small pot over medium-high heat. Add minced onion (you’ll use the diced onion in step 5), remaining garlic,
      and a pinch of salt; cook, stirring, until fragrant, 30 seconds. • Add bulgur; cook, stirring, until toasted, 1-2 minutes. • Stir in stock concentrates, 1 cup water ,
      and ½ tsp salt (1 tsp for 4). Cover, bring to a boil, then reduce to a low simmer. Cook, covered, until water has absorbed and bulgur is tender, 12-15 minutes. • Turn off heat;
      keep covered until ready to serve.
    3. In a small bowl, combine sour cream, yogurt, reserved garlic, a big pinch of lemon zest, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Add water 1 tsp at a time
      until mixture reaches a drizzling consistency. Season with salt and pepper.
    4. Toss diced onion on one side of a baking sheet with a drizzle of oil, half the Za’atar Spice, a big pinch of salt,
      and pepper. Place tomatoes cut sides up on empty side of same sheet; season with salt and pepper. Roast on top rack until onion is tender and tomatoes are softened slightly but still
      retain their shape, 6-8 minutes. (TIP: If water pools in tomato halves after roasting, blot dry with paper towels.)
    5. Meanwhile, pat chicken* dry with paper towels; season all over with remaining Za'atar Spice, salt, and pepper. • Heat a drizzle of oil
      in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken; cook until browned and cooked through, 3-5 minutes per side. Remove from heat; set aside to rest.
    6. Fluff bulgur with a fork. Stir in roasted onion, half the parsley, remaining lemon zest, a big squeeze of lemon juice, and 1 TBSP butter
      until butter melts. Season with salt and pepper if desired
    7. Slice chicken crosswise. • Stuff tomatoes with as much bulgur filling as will fit. Divide remaining
      filling on one side of each plate and place stuffed tomatoes on top. Place chicken next to stuffed tomatoes. Drizzle everything with sauce. Garnish with remaining parsley and serve with any
      remaining lemon wedges on the side.
